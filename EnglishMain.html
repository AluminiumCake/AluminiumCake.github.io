About Me

I was born in Belarus. I have been living in Poland for several years.

I speak several languages ​​- Belarusian, Russian, English, Polish, and Spanish. I also understand most Slavic languages.

I started writing music in January 2024 and released my first release - Farsh.

My first VOCALOID song was I'm Always Here To Support You feat. MEIKO.

My favourite vocal-synth is OpenUTAU. My favorite voicebanks are MEIKO, Uta Utane, and Xingchen Stardust.

I first registered on YouTube on May 20, 2018, my birthday. This is the channel.

I love playing video games. My favourites are Mount&Blade, Geometry Dash, Half-Life and Spore.

In elementary and middle school, I went to music school. I played percussion instruments - the xylophone and drums.

My favorite musical instruments are the violin, piano, and drums.

I listen to everything I like; genre doesn't make sense. My favorite VOCALOID producer is EZFG, my favorite VOCALOID song is Kimi to boku no resonance.

I like to do everything alone. I dislike having to do something in a group of more than three people.
